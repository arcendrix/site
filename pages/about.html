About Arcendrix

Arcendrix is built around one idea: the best websites feel obvious. They’re fast, calm, and immediately understandable — by humans and machines.

Principles

  • Clarity wins: one page, one job.
  • Speed is trust: keep payloads lean.
  • Accessibility is not optional: keyboard-first, readable contrast, semantic HTML.
  • Structured data: help search engines and AI interpret your intent.

What you get

Templates

Global header/footer, consistent layout, and reusable components.

Image bank

A searchable gallery powered by a JSON index — simple, fast, editable.

SEO baseline

Meta tags, OpenGraph, sitemap, robots, and JSON-LD out of the box.
